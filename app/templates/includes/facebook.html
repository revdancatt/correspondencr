How to find the Facebook Webcal feed

To grab all the birthdays from Facebook you'll need to get to the webcal feed, to do that you first need to click on the Events link over on the left hand side of the page.

 

Then scroll down, down, down the right hand side until you see the whole "You can add your events to Microsoft Outlook, Google Calendar or Apple Calendar. Once you add them, they'll stay updated" panel. Right click on the "Birthdays" link and select "Copy Link Address", see the image below for help.

The link will look something like webcal://www.facebook.com/ical/b.php?uid=000000000000000&key=AAAAAAAAAAAAAAAA

Copy that and paste it into the form above.
